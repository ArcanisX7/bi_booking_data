Year-on-Year by Market & Channel (Oct 2025 vs Oct 2024)
Compares the last fully closed month (October 2025) to the same month one year earlier. November 2025 is excluded because it is incomplete in the source data. AOV is calculated as GBV divided by Bookings, not averaged, so it stays accurate at every level (Channel, Market, and Grand Total).

Developments over the Last 6 Months (May–Oct 2025 vs. 2024)
Shows May–October 2025 against the same months in 2024, month by month. A single month can be noisy for smaller segments, so this view gives a fuller picture of recent momentum. Each month's year-on-year change is shown alongside its raw value.

Growth Index & Contribution to Growth (YTD Jan–Oct)
Uses a longer year-to-date window (January–October) instead of one month, because smaller markets can swing sharply month to month. A Growth Index of 100 means a market is growing at the same pace as the whole portfolio; above 100 is faster, below 100 is slower. Contribution to Growth shows what share of the portfolio's total absolute growth came from that market.
